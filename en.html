About Us

Specialised custom aluminium, glass and ceramic solutions.

Frontal is a specialised workshop for producing custom aluminium, glass and ceramic solutions for residential and commercial spaces.

We create elements such as sliding doors, display cabinets, ceramic worktops, kitchen islands and other interior solutions. Each project is adapted to the dimensions of the space and the client’s wishes.

We combine premium materials with precise craftsmanship to ensure the result is durable, functional and visually aligned with the space.
